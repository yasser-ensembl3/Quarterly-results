# YETI Holdings, Inc. - Strategic Analysis
**Quarter:** Q3 2025

## Executive Summary

**TL;DR:** YETI delivered strong international and Coolers & Equipment growth while managing through Drinkware headwinds in the US, with management expressing confidence in long-term high single to low double-digit growth potential.

**Management Tone:** Optimistic

Management emphasized YETI's diversified strategy is working with robust international expansion, strong product innovation pipeline, and effective omni-channel approach. Despite promotional pressures in US Drinkware, the company sees momentum building across strategic growth pillars with significant global opportunity ahead.
