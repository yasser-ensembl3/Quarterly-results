# Coinbase Global, Inc. - Strategic Analysis
**Quarter:** Q3 2025

## Executive Summary

**TL;DR:** Coinbase delivered strong Q3 results while advancing its Everything Exchange vision through expanded derivatives offerings, the Deribit acquisition, and accelerating stablecoin adoption amid improving regulatory clarity.

**Management Tone:** Optimistic

Management emphasizes the substantial risks inherent in supporting cryptocurrency assets and blockchain networks, including technical vulnerabilities, regulatory uncertainties, and the challenges of operating in a rapidly evolving technological landscape. The company acknowledges significant exposure to third-party risks and the need for substantial compliance investments.
