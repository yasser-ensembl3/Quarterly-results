# Amazon.com, Inc. - Financial Analysis
**Quarter:** Q4 2025
**Currency:** USD
**Sector:** ecommerce

## Executive Financial Summary

Amazon delivered Q4 2025 revenue of $213.4B, up 14% YoY (12% FX-adjusted), driven by AWS re-acceleration to 24% growth and broad-based strength across all segments. Operating income reached $25.0B (11.7% margin), though this includes $2.4B in special charges; adjusted operating income would have been $27.4B. Net income was $21.2B ($1.95 diluted EPS), up 6% YoY, while free cash flow declined 71% to $11.2B TTM due to massive AI infrastructure investments.
