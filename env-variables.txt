================================================================================
                           HOW TO GET EACH KEY
================================================================================

GOOGLE_SERVICE_ACCOUNT_JSON:
  1. Go to https://console.cloud.google.com
  2. Select your project (or create one)
  3. Go to "IAM & Admin" > "Service Accounts"
  4. Click "Create Service Account"
  5. Give it a name, click "Create and Continue"
  6. Grant role: "Editor" or custom Drive access
  7. Click "Done"
  8. Click on the service account > "Keys" tab
  9. "Add Key" > "Create new key" > JSON
  10. Download the JSON file
  11. Copy its content to GOOGLE_SERVICE_ACCOUNT_JSON (single line)

  IMPORTANT: Share your Google Drive folder with the service account email!
  (the client_email from the JSON, e.g., myaccount@myproject.iam.gserviceaccount.com)

GDRIVE_ROOT_FOLDER_ID:
  Open your Google Drive folder in browser
  URL: https://drive.google.com/drive/folders/1ABC123xyz
  The ID is: 1ABC123xyz

OPENAI_API_KEY:
  https://platform.openai.com/api-keys

ANTHROPIC_API_KEY:
  https://console.anthropic.com/settings/keys
